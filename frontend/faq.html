Network
How do I reset my router?
▾
Hold the reset button on the back of your router for 10–15 seconds using a pin until the lights flash. The router restarts with factory defaults. Any custom Wi-Fi names or passwords will be erased.
Why is my internet slow?
▾
Common causes include router placement, too many connected devices, or active large downloads. Restart your router, move it to a central location, and pause any streaming or backups. Run a speed test at fast.com to check your current speeds.
How do I connect a new device to Wi-Fi?
▾
Open Wi-Fi settings on your device, select your network name, and enter your password. If you don't know your password, it's usually printed on the back of your router.
My Wi-Fi keeps disconnecting
▾
Try updating your router firmware via the manufacturer's website, and switch to a less congested Wi-Fi channel (1, 6, or 11 for 2.4GHz). If it persists, contact your ISP to check for line issues.
How do I improve my Wi-Fi signal?
▾
Place your router in a central, elevated location away from walls and appliances. Consider a Wi-Fi extender or mesh system for larger spaces. Switching to the 5GHz band also provides faster speeds at shorter range.

Billing
How can I change my billing address?
▾
Log in to your account, go to Settings → Billing, and click Edit next to your billing address. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
Why was I charged twice?
▾
Duplicate charges often appear as temporary authorisation holds that clear within 3–5 business days. If both charges are fully settled, contact our billing team with your order reference and we'll issue a refund immediately.
How do I update my payment method?
▾
Go to Settings → Billing → Payment Methods and click Add new card. Once saved, set it as the default before your next billing date.
How do I cancel my subscription?
▾
Go to Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription → Cancel. Your access continues until the end of your paid period. You'll receive a confirmation email — check spam if it doesn't arrive within 10 minutes.
Can I get a refund?
▾
We offer refunds within 30 days of purchase. Contact our billing team with your order number and reason. We'll review and process eligible refunds within 3 business days.

Account
How do I reset my password?
▾
Click Forgot password on the login page and enter your email. A reset link arrives within a few minutes — check spam if it doesn't appear. The link expires after 30 minutes.
Can I have multiple users on one account?
▾
Yes — on Team and Business plans, invite users under Settings → Team. Each gets their own login and can be assigned a role: Admin, Agent, or Viewer.
How do I update my email address?
▾
Go to Settings → Profile and click the edit icon next to your email. You'll need to verify both the old and new addresses before the change saves.
How do I enable two-factor authentication?
▾
Go to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication and click Enable. Use an authenticator app (Google Authenticator or Authy) or SMS. Save your backup codes in a secure place.
How do I delete my account?
▾
Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account and type DELETE to confirm. All data is permanently removed within 30 days. Active subscriptions are cancelled and no further charges are made.

Device
My device won't turn on
▾
Hold the power button for 10 seconds to force restart. Check the charger connection and confirm the outlet works. If it still won't power on after 30 minutes of charging, contact our hardware support team.
How do I update my device firmware?
▾
Open Settings → About → Software Update on your device. Connect to Wi-Fi and ensure at least 50% battery before starting. The update typically takes 5–15 minutes.
My device is running slowly
▾
Restart the device, close unused apps, and check available storage. If storage is under 10% free, clear old files or apps. Running a firmware update can also resolve performance issues.
